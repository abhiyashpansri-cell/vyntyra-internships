Resume Link Support

How to upload resume to Google Drive and share in public view

Part 1: Upload your resume to Google Drive

  1. Open Google Drive: go to https://drive.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Click the + New button on the left panel.
  3. Select File upload and choose your resume file (preferably PDF format).
  4. Wait for the upload progress to complete. You should see the file in your Drive list.
  5. Rename your file clearly if needed, for example: YourName_Resume.pdf.

Part 2: Make the file publicly viewable

  1. Right-click the uploaded resume file in Google Drive.
  2. Click Share.
  3. In the share dialog, find General access at the bottom.
  4. Change access from Restricted to Anyone with the link.
  5. Set permission to Viewer only (do not use Editor).
  6. Click Copy link, then click Done.

Part 3: Verify and paste the correct link

  1. Open an incognito/private browser window.
  2. Paste the copied link and check that your resume opens without sign-in permission errors.
  3. If you see "Access denied", re-open Share settings and confirm Anyone with the link - Viewer.
  4. Return to this form and paste the same link into Paste Your Resume Link.
  5. Complete payment, then submit your application.
